# Order Up — Consumer App Requirements

## Overview

A complete digital dining experience for your customers whether they want delivery, takeaway, or dine-in service. Customers can browse your menu, place orders in seconds, reserve tables, track deliveries, and enjoy a seamless mobile-first experience that keeps them coming back.

---

## Key Features

🍽️ Digital Restaurant Menu
🛵 Delivery Ordering
🥡 Takeout & Pickup
🍷 Dine-In Experience
🛒 Fast Checkout Experience
🔔 Real-Time Notifications
❤️ Favorites & Quick Reorders
⭐ Reviews & Ratings
🎟️ Promotions & Discount Campaigns
📱 iOS & Android Mobile Apps
💳 Payment Integration
📊 Restaurant Management Dashboard (Free)

---

## Core Features

- [ ] **User Registration / Login** — email, phone, social auth
- [ ] **Location-Based Search** — find nearby restaurants
- [ ] **Restaurant Discovery** — browse by cuisine, rating, price, availability
- [ ] **Real-Time Table Availability** — view available slots
- [ ] **Online Table Booking** — select date, time, party size
- [ ] **Waitlist Management** — join waitlist when fully booked
- [ ] **Special Requests** — dietary restrictions, seating preferences (window, outdoor, etc.)
- [ ] **QR Menu** — contactless digital menu scanning
- [ ] **In-App Payments** — pay for reservations, pre-orders, deposits
- [ ] **Booking History** — past and upcoming reservations
- [ ] **Push Notifications** — booking confirmations, reminders, offers
- [ ] **Loyalty & Rewards** — points, cashback, exclusive offers
- [ ] **Ratings & Reviews** — rate dining experience

---

## Technical Requirements

- Cross-platform: iOS & Android (Expo / React Native)
- Real-time updates for table availability
- Secure payment processing
- Push notification service
- Image storage for menus and restaurant photos
- GDPR compliant
